# English Abstract

Youth unemployment is one of the greatest challenges facing modern African societies, threatening economic stability and social cohesion. With millions of young people entering the job market each year, traditional employment avenues cannot keep up. This presentation explores the roots and impact of youth unemployment and makes a case for digital skills training and entrepreneurship as scalable, sustainable solutions. Showcasing real African success stories, we demonstrate how empowering youth with technology and entrepreneurial mindsets can turn a crisis into an opportunity for growth and innovation.

# Kiswahili Abstract

Uwasilishaji huu unachunguza mizizi na athari za tatizo hili na kupendekeza mafunzo ya ujuzi wa kidijitali na ujasiriamali kama suluhisho la kudumu. Kwa mifano halisi ya mafanikio kutoka Afrika, tunaonyesha jinsi kuwawezesha vijana kwa teknolojia na fikra za ujasiriamali kunaweza kubadili changamoto kuwa fursa ya maendeleo.
