# English Abstract

Progressive Web Applications (PWAs) represent a breakthrough in how we deliver digital experiences in the mobile-first era. PWAs combine the universality of the web with the performance and rich features of native mobile apps, offering users enhanced reliability, speed, and engagement across any device. Built using modern web standards such as Service Workers and Web App Manifests, PWAs support offline usage, installation on home screens, and push notifications—crucial for accessibility in regions with spotty connectivity. Their ability to bridge digital divides, lower development costs, and foster inclusion makes them especially powerful for Africa’s growing technology landscape. This presentation unpacks the technology, impact, and future of PWAs, drawing from both global examples and personal project experience to demonstrate their transformative potential in Computer Science.

# Kiswahili Abstract

Programu Tovuti Zinazoendelea (PWAs) ni uvumbuzi mkubwa katika jinsi tunavyotoa huduma za kidijitali katika dunia ya simu za kisasa. PWAs zinaunganisha urahisi wa tovuti na utendaji wa juu wa programu za simu, zikiwapa watumiaji uzoefu wa kuaminika, kasi na mwingiliano kwenye kifaa chochote. Kwa kutumia teknolojia za kisasa kama Service Workers na Web App Manifests, PWAs zinawezesha matumizi hata bila mtandao, usakinishaji kwenye skrini nyumbani, na arifa za papo kwa papo—vitu muhimu katika maeneo yenye mtandao hafifu. Uwezo wa PWAs kukuza ujumuishaji wa kidijitali na kupunguza gharama za maendeleo ni muhimu sana kwa Afrika inapoendelea kidijitali. Uwasilishaji huu utachambua teknolojia, athari, na mustakabali wa PWAs, kwa mifano ya kimataifa na uzoefu binafsi, ukionyesha namna zinavyobadilisha tasnia ya Sayansi ya Kompyuta.

# Kikuyu Abstract

PWAs (Progressive Web Applications) nĩ gwĩkũyũrũ kwa wendo wa gũcaria mũno wa kũcokia mĩthirirĩrĩ ya digitari thirikari-inĩ ya rũciaro wa maũndũ ma mobile. PWAs nĩgũtuĩrwo na thirikari ya web na utũmia wa gũkĩnyĩrĩra hamwe na mũthenya wa program cia mobile, nĩguo mũtumia agĩthondeka harĩ gihũtĩ, haraka, na gũtũma na igũrũ rĩrĩa acio. Kũgũtĩkira Service Workers na Web App Manifest, PWAs nĩgũtuo kũgũtĩkira harĩa hakuirwo na neti, gũtũma home screen, na notification cia mũno - nii matuĩke na thirikari-inĩ ya Kenya na Africa. Nĩguo twienda gũkũrũma ciana, kũtũma ithui mbarũri, na gũtũma wendo wa digitari. Ucemeo ũyũ nĩgwenda gũcokia mĩthirirĩrĩ ya tũria na magũrũrũ, na gũcaria maũndũ mahinda-inĩ wa Sayansi ya Kompyuta.
